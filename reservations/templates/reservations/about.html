How Our Booking Process Works

1

Visit the Booking Page

Start by visiting our booking page to begin your journey. You'll be able to plan your trip from there.

2

Select Your Travel Cities

Choose the cities you want to travel between. You can easily select your origin and destination from the dropdown menus.

3

Specify the Number of Seats

Enter the number of seats you require for your trip. You can choose the number based on your travel needs.

4

Choose Bed Preferences

Decide if you prefer a seat or a bed for your trip. You can select your preference to ensure maximum comfort during your journey.

5

Choose Travel Preferences

You can also specify whether you prefer a shorter distance or less travel time. This will help us find the best option for your needs.

6

Click "Search Trains"

Once you've entered your preferences, simply click the search button to find the best train options available for your travel needs.

7

Select the Best Train Option

Browse the available train options and select the best one that suits your preferences, including schedule and pricing.

8

Click on the Pay Button

Once you've chosen your preferred option, click the "Pay" button to proceed with the payment for your bookings.

9

Proceed to Payment

Follow the prompts to complete your payment. After successful payment, your bookings will be confirmed and you're all set for your trip!

Our booking process is designed to be simple and seamless, so you can focus on what matters most—your travel. We’re here to help make your journey as comfortable and efficient as possible.
